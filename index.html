Rules

Game Overview

This game is an expansion of the classic Rock, Paper, Scissors game. It adds two more options: Lizard and Spock, creating a game with five choices instead of three.

How to play

  1. Two players simultaneously choose one of five hand gestures representing rock, paper, scissors, lizard, or Spock.
  2. The winner is determined by comparing the chosen gestures according to the rules below.

Winning combinations

Each gesture can defeat two other gestures and be defeated by two others:

  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Spock vaporizes Rock

Determining the winner

  • If both players choose the same gesture, it's a tie.
  • Otherwise, the winner is determined by the above combinations.
